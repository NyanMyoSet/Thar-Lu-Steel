About Us

Thar Lu Steel Company Limited is dedicated to contributing to Myanmar's economic and urban development by providing high-quality steel roofing sheets and construction materials. With a focus on sustainable growth, we are committed to modernizing the Thanlyin GI Sheet factory to increase production capacity and meet the growing domestic demand, which has been observed to increase by 5% to 7% annually.

Our mission is to become a key player in the Myanmar steel industry by leveraging advanced technology and strategic investments to produce a diverse range of products, including modern items like Decking Sheets, Lip Channels, and Pipe Sections. By revitalizing local manufacturing, we aim to reduce reliance on imports, create job opportunities, and build a more resilient national economy.

Our Products

Galvanized Iron Sheet

Our high-quality GI Sheets are designed for durability and strength, perfect for roofing and cladding in a variety of construction projects.

PPGI 4-Angle Sheet

Pre-painted for a vibrant finish and extra protection, our PPGI 4-Angle Sheets combine aesthetics with robust structural integrity.

Decking Sheet

Ideal for multi-story buildings and construction, our decking sheets provide a strong and reliable base for concrete floors.

Lip Channel

Versatile and lightweight, our Lip Channels are perfect for various applications, including solar panel racks, storage shelves, and ceiling support structures.

Hollow Pipes & Tubes

Designed for structural integrity and ease of use, our hollow pipes and tubes are essential for a wide range of building and fabrication needs.

Polycarbonate Sheet
