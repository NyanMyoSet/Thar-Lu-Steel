About Us

Thar Lu Steel Company Limited is dedicated to contributing to Myanmar's economic and urban development by providing high-quality steel roofing sheets and construction materials. With a focus on sustainable growth, we are committed to modernizing the Thanlyin GI Sheet factory to increase production capacity and meet the growing domestic demand, which has been observed to increase by 5% to 7% annually.

Our mission is to become a key player in the Myanmar steel industry by leveraging advanced technology and strategic investments to produce a diverse range of products, including modern items like Decking Sheets, Lip Channels, and Pipe Sections. By revitalizing local manufacturing, we aim to reduce reliance on imports, create job opportunities, and build a more resilient national economy.
